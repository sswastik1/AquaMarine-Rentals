The Super Air Nautique 210

Boat Overview

Load up on ballast and crank up your favourite music.You are about to be launched by the legend – the boat that started the big-air revolution - the Super Air Nautique 210. The Super Air Nautique 210 features a redesigned deck with sizzling style, including a trick new stern seat that can be faced forward or aft, folds down to create an entry step with cup holders and is removable. Change the wake in a heartbeat - right from the cockpit – with Hydro-Gate™, a Correct Craft exclusive. There’s also a new stern-mounted pop-up pylon. You want options? We’ve got all the latest, including the spanking new, polished stainless steel Titan Tower that’s lighter and easier to fold down, plus a redesigned spinner rack, new lightweight tower speakers, and more. Peaky wakes with lips that kick, minimal trough, radical air. At Nautiques by Correct Craft, our standards just keep getting higher and higher.
