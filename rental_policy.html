General Rental Information

  • All rates are in Canadian dollars.
  • GST (Goods and Services Tax) and gasoline are not included in the rental rates.
  • You will get a completely filled fuel tank and you need to return the boat in exactly same fuel level.
  • You must pay the deposit in order to guarantee a reservation.
  • Life jackets are included with all boats.
  • Always ensure that your booking is confirmed; which is when you get a confirmation email from our official email.

Cancellations

  • Customer won’t be charged any money for cancellations made upto 30 days’ notice before the reservation date.
  • Cancellation made within (48) hour notice will result in a 50% charge and not showing up would result in will paying Aquamarine Rentals 100% of the rental fee
  • Worse luck, no refund is given due to weather conditions.

Pick-up and Return Policy

  • One should try to be punctual to the time specified for picking up and dropping off the boat in the booking agreement.
  • Both the pick-up time and drop off times should be tried to maintain. As a late fee of $70.00 would be charged per half-hour for late drop-offs by the customer.
  • One should have a valid driver’s license and credit card. Person who’s credit card is being used should be physically present when picking up the boat.
  • You will get a completely filled fuel tank and you need to return the boat in exactly same fuel level.
  • Extending reservations may be made at any time subject to availability.
  • A pre-authorized damage deposit of $2000 is required for Four Winns 190 and $1200 for Sea Doo.

Reservation Policy

  • Reservations must be made for rentals of one full day or more for the special rates to apply.
  • A 20% deposit is required when making any reservation.
  • The minimum rental age for Four Winns 190 is 25 years, and for Sea Doo is 21 years; with a valid driver’s license and a credit card. One can extend his/her reservation at any time subject to availability.
  • We assure you to provide the best service and the boat you desire at the best rates.
